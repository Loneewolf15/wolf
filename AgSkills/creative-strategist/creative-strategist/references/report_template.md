# Brand Strategy Report Template

Use this template structure for all detailed brand strategy reports. Adapt section depth based on scope.

---

# [Report Title]

## 1. Executive Summary

One-paragraph overview: the brand's current position, core challenge, and top-line strategic recommendation.

## 2. Brand Audit & Current Positioning

- **Brand perception** — How the market currently sees the brand
- **Competitive landscape** — Key competitors and their positioning
- **SWOT snapshot** — Strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, threats (brief table)
- **Visual & verbal identity assessment** — Logo, color palette, typography, tone of voice

## 3. Target Audience Analysis

Break into defined segments. For each:

| Segment | Demographics | Psychographics | Pain Points | Preferred Channels |
|---------|-------------|----------------|-------------|-------------------|
| Tech-Savvy Professionals | 25-40, urban, high income | Early adopters, value innovation | Fragmented ecosystem, trust | Twitter/X, Product Hunt, tech blogs |
| Youth (Gen Z) | 16-25, digital native | Status-conscious, sustainability | Authenticity, affordability | TikTok, Instagram, YouTube Shorts |
| Transporters | 28-55, logistics/mobility | Efficiency-driven, cost-sensitive | Reliability, downtime cost | WhatsApp, trade publications |

Include empathy maps or persona summaries where valuable.

## 4. Strategic Recommendations

Number each strategy. For each:

### Strategy [N]: [Title]

- **Objective** — What it achieves
- **Tactic** — How to execute
- **Target segment** — Who it impacts
- **Inspiration** — Reference case study (Apple, Microsoft, Xiaomi, Netflix, etc.)
- **Expected impact** — Measurable outcome (e.g., +15% engagement, +20% brand recall)
- **Timeline** — Short/Medium/Long term

## 5. Brand Experience Case Studies

For each referenced company, use this structure:

### [Company Name]

- **Challenge** — What they faced
- **Strategy** — What they did
- **Execution** — Key tactics
- **Result** — Measurable outcomes
- **Lesson for client** — Transferable insight

## 6. Customer Engagement Framework

- **Engagement funnel** — Awareness → Interest → Desire → Action → Advocacy
- **Touchpoint mapping** — Key brand touchpoints and optimization plan
- **Content strategy** — Content types, frequency, channels per segment
- **Community building** — Loyalty programs, user-generated content, ambassador programs
- **Innovative approaches** — AR/VR experiences, gamification, personalization engines

## 7. KPIs & Measurable Outcomes

| KPI | Current Baseline | Target | Timeframe |
|-----|-----------------|--------|-----------|
| Brand awareness | X% | Y% | 6 months |
| Customer engagement rate | X% | Y% | 3 months |
| Market share | X% | Y% | 12 months |
| NPS score | X | Y | 6 months |
| Conversion rate | X% | Y% | 6 months |

## 8. Implementation Roadmap

### Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1-3)
- Quick wins and infrastructure

### Phase 2: Growth (Months 4-8)
- Core strategy deployment

### Phase 3: Scale (Months 9-12)
- Market expansion and optimization

## 9. Budget Allocation (if applicable)

High-level percentage breakdown across channels and initiatives.

## 10. Appendix

Supporting data, extended research, additional case studies.
